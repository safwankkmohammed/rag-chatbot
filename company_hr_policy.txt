Company HR Policy Manual

1. Working Hours
The standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to work 40 hours per week.
Flexible working hours may be allowed with manager approval.

2. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to the following leaves:
- Annual Leave: 20 days per year
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Casual Leave: 5 days per year
Unused leaves may be carried forward up to 10 days.

3. Public Holidays
The company observes all national and regional public holidays.
A holiday calendar will be shared at the beginning of each year.

4. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with prior approval.
Full-time remote work requires special approval from management.

5. Attendance Policy
Employees must mark attendance daily using the company system.
Late arrival beyond 15 minutes will be considered half-day after 3 occurrences.

6. Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professionalism, integrity, and respect.
Harassment, discrimination, and misconduct are strictly prohibited.

7. Dress Code
Business casual attire is required.
On Fridays, employees may wear smart casuals.

8. Overtime Policy
Overtime must be pre-approved by the manager.
Compensation will be provided as per company guidelines.

9. Employee Benefits
Employees are eligible for:
- Health insurance coverage
- Provident fund contributions
- Annual performance bonus

10. Probation Period
New employees undergo a probation period of 6 months.
Confirmation depends on performance evaluation.

11. Termination Policy
Employees must provide 30 days’ notice before resignation.
The company reserves the right to terminate employment based on misconduct or poor performance.

12. Data Security Policy
Employees must not share confidential company data.
Violation may lead to disciplinary action.

13. Training and Development
Employees are encouraged to participate in training programs.
The company provides learning resources and workshops.

14. Grievance Handling
Employees can report issues to HR confidentially.
All grievances will be addressed within a reasonable time.

15. Travel Policy
Business travel must be approved in advance.
Expenses will be reimbursed as per company policy.
